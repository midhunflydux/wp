Garden Cottage and Walled Garden, Lanwades Park, Kentford, Newmarket, Suffolk, CB8 7UU


Type: Leisure, Development Opportunity - commercial, Development Opportunity - residential, Land - Commercial, Retail
Tenure: For Sale
Size: 0.73 to 2.48 Acres
(0.3 to 1 Hectares)
Price: Freehold £350,000 - £750,000 for the Freehold

Description

The Walled Garden and Garden Cottage are situated in a parkland setting neighbouring Lanwades Hall. The grounds extend to approximately 2.48 acres (STS) located just off the A14 between Newmarket and Bury St Edmunds.

Lot 1 - The Garden Cottage sits within a site of 0.73 acres, the three bedroom cottage comes with attractive gardens and a 0.45 acre paddock.

Lot 2 - The Walled Garden sits within a site of around 1.76 acres and provides 4,285 sq ft of outbuildings, kennels and storage areas.

The freehold is available together or separately with vacant possession on a conditional or unconditional basis.
