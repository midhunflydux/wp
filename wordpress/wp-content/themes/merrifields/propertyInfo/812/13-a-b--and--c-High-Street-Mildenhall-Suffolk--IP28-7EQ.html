13 a, b & c High Street, Mildenhall, Suffolk, IP28 7EQ


Type: Retail
Tenure: To Let
Size: 409 to 1612 Sq Ft
(38 to 149.75 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £6,000 - £15,000

Description

Mildenhall is a market town located adjacent to the A11 close to the Norfolk/Suffolk border.

The shop is prominently located with a traditional glazed shop front facing the High Street. Other retail occupiers on the High Street include; A1 shops, A5 takeaways and A3 cafes.

The shop is to be divided into 3 self-contained retail units each with an exclusive shop front, entrance and WC. The shops are available to let on terms to be agreed together or individually.
