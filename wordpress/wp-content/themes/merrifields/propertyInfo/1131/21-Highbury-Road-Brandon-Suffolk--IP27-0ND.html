Description

The property is situated at the entrance to London Road Industrial Estate, south west of Brandon Town centre and 5 miles North West of Thetford & the A11 trunk road.

The 59,136 sq ft distribution premises comprises a detached twin bay warehouse with 2 storey office block to the front. Externally there is staff and customer parking and a fenced yard and loading area.

The warehouse is available for let as whole or in part on terms to be agreed or the whole is available for sale freehold.
