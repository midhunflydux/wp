Property Details

30 Bridge Street, Thetford, Norfolk, IP24 3AG


Type: Office
Tenure: To Let
Size: 1198 to 1368 Sq Ft
(111.29 to 127.09 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £12,000 Per Annum

Description

30 Bridge Street is located on the edge of Thetford town centre off the Bury Road and is currently occupied by Knights Lowe Accountants on the ground floor.

The Grade II listed office space includes a reception area, kitchenette, conference room, 3 offices and WC on the ground floor and 5 offices with WCs on the first floor.

There is also parking available to the front and rear of the property. The specification also includes, CAT II lighting, carpetting and perimeter trunking.
