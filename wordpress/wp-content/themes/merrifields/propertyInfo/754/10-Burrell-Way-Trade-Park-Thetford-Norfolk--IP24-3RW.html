Property Details

10, Burrell Way Trade Park, Thetford, Norfolk, IP24 3RW


Type: Industrial Warehouse
Tenure: To Let
Size: 35967 Sq Ft
(3341.33 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold On Application

Description

The warehouse is located on the Burrell Way Trade Park off the London Road close to the A11 trunk road in Thetford Norfolk.

It comprises a detached warehouse of steel frame construction with metal cladding under a double pitched roof, with a small office pod to the front and internal offices and ancillary facilities. The GIA is about 35,967 sq ft.

To the front of the property is an enclosed yard of about 25m x 90m and there is an additional enclosed yard/car park to the rear of about 17.5m x 90m.

The building has been fully refurbished and is ready for immediate occupation and is available for let on terms to be agreed.
