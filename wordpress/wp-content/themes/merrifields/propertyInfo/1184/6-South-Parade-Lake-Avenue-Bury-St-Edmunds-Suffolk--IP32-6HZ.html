6 South Parade, Lake Avenue, Bury St. Edmunds, Suffolk, IP32 6HZ


Type: Retail
Tenure: To Let
Size: 551 Sq Ft
(51.19 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £8,000 Per Annum

Description

The shop is located on Lake Avenue in the heart of the Howard Estate in Bury St Edmunds. The Estate is accessed off Mildenhall Road.

The ground floor shop is part of a neighbourhood parade providing local services. Adjacent occupiers include; a convenience store, a hairdressers and takeaways. The shop includes an exclusive shop front and entrance, rear stores/office and WC.

The shop will be refurbished ready for tenants occupation and is available to let on terms to be agreed.
