9, Burrell Way Trade Park, Burrell Way, Thetford, Norfolk, IP24 3QS


Type: Industrial Warehouse
Tenure: To Let
Size: 8564 Sq Ft

Price: Leasehold £39,800 Per Annum

Description

The Burrell Way Trade Park is located off the London Road in Thetford close to the A11. The unit sits adjacent to other industrial and warehousing operations.

The unit is detached measuring approximately 8,564 sq ft and sits on a good sized site with external storage areas and vehicle maneuvering space. The unit provides mostly open plan storage space with level loading from three roller shutter doors, a loading area and has an office and WC's.

The unit is available on a new lease for a term to be agreed.
