Suffolk Meat Traders, Grove Lane, Elmswell, Bury St. Edmunds, Suffolk, IP30 9HN


Type: Industrial Warehouse
Tenure: For Sale
Size: 1391.22 Sq M
(14975.09 Sq Ft)
Price: Freehold £750,000 for the Freehold

Description

A well equipped and meat / food production and packing facility fully fitted and ready for use. The site is located in the Grove Lane Industrial area just outside Elmswell with road links to the A14 between Bury St Edmunds and Stowmarket.

The site comprises a production and packing facility of approximately 11,971 sq ft and a two storey office block of 3,004 sq ft. The yard is layed with a combination of concrete for the loading areas and further hardstanding for parking and external storage.

The site is available immediately freehold with vacant possession.
