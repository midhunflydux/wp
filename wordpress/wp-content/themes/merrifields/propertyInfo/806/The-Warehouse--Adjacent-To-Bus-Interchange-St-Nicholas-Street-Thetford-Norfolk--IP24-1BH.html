Property Details

The Warehouse , Adjacent To Bus Interchange, St. Nicholas Street, Thetford, Norfolk, IP24 1BH


Type: Office, Retail
Tenure: For Sale, To Let
Size: 2248 to 3766 Sq Ft
(208.84 to 349.86 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £22,500 Per Annum
Freehold On Application

Description

The building is located adjacent to the Bus Interchange in Thetford benefiting from good pedestrian traffic into the Town Centre from bus users.

The Grade II listed former Charles Burrell Warehouse has been refurbished following a fire to a shell, providing open plan ground floor and first floor business space (subject to fitting of stairs).

The building has a ground floor gross internal area of 2,248 sq ft with a potential GIA of 3,766 sq ft (subject to staircase being fitted). The building is available to let on terms to be agreed or for sale freehold.
