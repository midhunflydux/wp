Description

The site is located off Caxton Way in Thetford around 0.5 miles from A11 with a site area of approximately 8 acres.

This strategically located site has been cleared and levelled ready for development. Outline planning permission has been granted for 150,000 sq ft of Industrial / Warehouse / Distribution space with 12m eaves height.

The site is fully serviced and ready for development. Available for developers or freehold occupiers and suitable for B8, B1 and B2 uses STPP.
