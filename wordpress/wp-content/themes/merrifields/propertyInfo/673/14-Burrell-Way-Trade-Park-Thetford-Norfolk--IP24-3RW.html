14 Burrell Way Trade Park, Thetford, Norfolk, IP24 3RW


Type: Industrial Warehouse
Tenure: To Let
Size: 11349 Sq Ft
(1054.32 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £59,500 Per Annum

Description

Burrell Way Trade Park is located close to the A11 trunk road opposite the Forest Retail Park. Other occupiers on the park include Lidl, Screwfix, Howdens, Ridgeons and nearby occupiers include Sainsbury, McDonalds, B&Q, Topps Tiles and Kwik Fit.

The building is prominently located on a corner plot with two vehicle access points to the side and rear and 23 car spaces. The yard is secure and mostly laid to concrete.

The building has been refurbished is about 11,349 sq ft on a site of about 1 acre and is available now on a new lease.
