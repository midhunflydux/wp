Property Details

Unit 30 & 42, Red House Farm, Earls Green, Bacton, Stowmarket, Suffolk, IP14 4LE


Type: Industrial Warehouse
Tenure: To Let
Size: 1900 to 4546 Sq Ft
(176.51 to 422.32 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £7,600 - £15,000 Per Annum

Description

Red House Farm is located on the edge of the village of Bacton, about 4 miles north (as the crow flies) of the A14 but with good access to the A14 at Stowmarket and Elmswell.

Units 30 and 42 are of timber/steel frame construction with a concrete floor under a fibre cement roof. The unit further benefits from two access doors an office and stores. The units are available now on a new lease individually or together.

Other occupiers within the Business Units at Red House Farm include joiners, fabricators, office users and supplier of portable buildings.
