Unit 47, Red House Farm, Earls Green, Bacton, Stowmarket, Suffolk, IP14 4LE


Type: Industrial Warehouse
Tenure: To Let
Size: 1419 to 3065 Sq Ft
(131.83 to 284.74 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £3,000 - £8,700 Per Annum

Description

Red House Farm is located on the edge of the village of Bacton, about 4 miles north (as the crow flies) of the A14 but with good access to the A14 at Stowmarket and Elmswell.

Unit 47 is of steel frame construction with, brick elevations, a concrete floor under a fibre cement roof. The unit has two access doors and a first floor storage areas which could adapted for office or other uses. The unit is available now on a new lease.

Other occupiers within Red House Farm Business Units include joiners, fabricators, office users and supplier of portable buildings.
