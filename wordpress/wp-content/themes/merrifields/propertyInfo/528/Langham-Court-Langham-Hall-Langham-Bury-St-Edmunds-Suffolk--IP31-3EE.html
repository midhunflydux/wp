Property Details

Langham Court, Langham Hall, Langham, Bury St. Edmunds, Suffolk, IP31 3EE


Type: Office
Tenure: To Let
Size: 1184 to 2569 Sq Ft
(109.99 to 238.66 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £20,300 - £28,250 Per Annum

Description

Langham Court has been sympathetically converted from a Coach House and provides essentially open plan office accommodation incorporating reception area, meeting room, open offices and a first floor office. The space is available immeadiately as a whole or possibly in parts.

It is located on the Langham Hall Estate in the village of Langham between Ixworth & Walsham le Willows and 5.5 miles from J47 of the A14 at Elmswell/Woolpit.

The specification includes oil central heating, 3phase electricity, CAT II lighting, period features, carpeting, two cloakrooms, kitchen, IT cabling, woodblock flooring, external patio and ample on site car parking. We are advised the local exchange is being "fibre enabled" in early 2018.
