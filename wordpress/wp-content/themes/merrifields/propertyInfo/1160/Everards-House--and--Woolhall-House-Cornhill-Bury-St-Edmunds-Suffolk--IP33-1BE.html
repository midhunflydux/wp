Everards House & Woolhall House, Cornhill, Bury St Edmunds, Suffolk, IP33 1BE


Type: Investment, Development Opportunity - commercial, Development Opportunity - residential, Investment - Office, Investment - Retail, Office
Tenure: For Sale
Size: 17547 Sq Ft
(1630.12 Sq M)
Price: Freehold On Application

Description

The property occupies an edge of prime location in the
centre of Bury St Edmunds, an attractive historic market town in the county of Suffolk. The three storey building provides retail accommodation on the ground floor which fronts onto both Cornhill and Woolhall Street. Two separate office entrances provide access to the First and Second floors providing office accommodation.

Tenants in the vicinity include Burger King, Bonmarche, Mind, British Heart Foundation and LP, Haart, Cafe Rouge, Iceland and other professional office occupiers.

The property is available freehold subject to the tenancies and vacant possession of part at an attractive Net Initial Yield of 8.5%.
