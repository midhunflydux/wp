Property Details

Hillier House, Norwich Road, Watton, Norfolk, IP25 6JX


Type: Office
Tenure: To Let
Size: 1742 to 4144 Sq Ft
(161.83 to 384.98 Sq M)
Price: Leasehold £20,000 Per Annum

Description

Hillier House is a small roadside business complex that has been improved to provide good quality office accommodation where the specification includes; Air cooling, IT cabling, Hardwood floors, Double glazing, Perimeter trunking, Carpeting and Window blinds.

There is ample on site car parking available and a significant lawned area which could be made available / developed to suit a particular tenants requirements.

The offices could be suitable for alternate uses. Other uses at Hillier House include; a gym, a children's play centre and a children's day nursery.
