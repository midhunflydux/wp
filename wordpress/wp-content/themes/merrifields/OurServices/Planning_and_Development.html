Planning and Development

Merrifields have a proven track record in providing advice to a range of clients including PLC’s, managed funds, limited companies and private individuals with regard to alternative uses. Our experienced surveyors have worked on a wide range of development opportunities providing advice on commercial, residential and rural properties.

It is imperative we understand our clients short and long-term objectives and the planning system, including local and national policies to give our clients the best possible advice. Sometimes that advice is against clients wants and needs, but is best advice to seek added value from clients assets.

In a world that is becoming very specialist and ‘legalistic’ Merrifields choose to work with specialists in their field including planning consultants, architects and environmental specialists in order to deliver cost effective, viable and profitable planning solutions.  We often head-up a multi-disciplined team to provide the desired result. Our planning and development services include:

• An initial review of existing and potential uses
• Existing use and future use site appraisal
• Sequential tests
• Appointment of architects, planning consultant and environmental consultants heading up a multi-disciplined team
• Joint venture advice
• Asset Disposal
