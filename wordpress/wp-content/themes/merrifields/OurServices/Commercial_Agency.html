Commercial Agency

Merrifields Agency Team acts for landlords, tenants and developers with disposal (letting and sale) and Acquisition (letting and sale) service which is tailored to the needs of the client and the property interest. We act for clients across the Eastern Region but particularly centred in the areas of West Suffolk, East Cambridgeshire, Breckland, Mid Suffolk and Babergh District Council areas. Our extensive local knowledge combined with a proven track record in sales, lettings and acquisitions gives us a distinct advantage in the marketplace.

Our Agency team works closely with our lease compliance and management teams where our services include:

• Sales, lettings and acquisition
• Investment sales
• Development and refurbishment advice
• Strategic bespoke marketing
• Negotiation of heads of agreement
• Liaison with legal teams to completion
