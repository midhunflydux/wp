Property Management

Our Property Management Team offers a range of services to manage commercial property effectively and efficiently.

We provide a bespoke service to meet Clients individual needs and look to maximise rent and capital values through a range of services including; rent collection, lease covenant compliance, service charge administration, service charge management, insurance advice and day to day tenant liaison.

The Management Team will work closely with, and/or part of, a multi-disciplined team, in-house or using external advisors, where specialist advice is required.

Our Property Management Services include:

• Asset Management
• Health and Safety Management
• Insurance Management
• Lease and Covenant Management
• Planned, Preventative and Reactive Maintenance
• Rent Collection and Client Statements
• Risk Assessments
• Service Charge Accounting and Management
• Tenant Liaison Management

We manage retail, office and industrial property, in both single and multiple occupancy, including mixed portfolios and individual properties for Private and Corporate Clients.
