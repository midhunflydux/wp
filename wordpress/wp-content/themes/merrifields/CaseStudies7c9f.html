Case Study

St Peters House and 1 Abbots Gate, Bury St Edmunds


Merrifields were appointed by Sicon Limited to find new premises to accommodate their expansion.  We successfully identified an office building and negotiated the acquisition of it.  Sicon hope to occupy their new office in Spring / Summer 2015.

Sicon have since instructed Merrifields to market their existing premises at 1 Abbots gate. Merrifields considered all options for the building including a letting, sale and possible conversion to residential use.   Following a decision to retain the building as a commercial investment Merrifields have commenced marketing with a view to finding a tenant to take a new full repairing and insuring lease.
