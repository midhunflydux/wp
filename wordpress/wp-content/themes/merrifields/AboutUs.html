Who we are

Merrifields was established over 25 years ago by Charles Merrifield to provide a professional service.  We are knowledgeable, approachable and adaptable and look to provide realistic commercial advice to add value to our Clients property assets throughout the Eastern region.  We look to develop relationships over years and decades. Merrifields are regulated by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors.

We have recently recruited two new employees, Mark Kohler and Gary Hubbard, to increase our capacity in line with ongoing growth & performance expectations.

Gordon Ellis is a Chartered Surveyor and Director of Merrifields.  Gordon previously worked for Lacy Scott and Sons and Gascoignes before joining Merrifields in 1993.  He is well known in the property market for his hands on practical approach.  When not in the office Gordon can be found at Bury St Edmunds Rugby Club.

William Dunkerley is a Chartered Surveyor and our Commercial Property Manager.  Will previously worked for Exeter City Council having obtained a degree from the University West of England in Bristol.  He is an experienced property manager with great attention to detail.  When not at the Rugby Club with Gordon Will enjoys mountain biking and can often be spotted in the beer gardens of Bury St Edmunds ale houses.

Rowan Mason is a Chartered Surveyor and joined the team after previously working for a regional Estate Agent.  Rowan completed his degree in 2006 from Nottingham Trent University.  He recently qualified as a member of the RICS.   In his spare time Rowan enjoys travelling, football, Glastonbury and is always up for a challenge!

Paul Robbins is a Chartered Surveyor who specialises in Valuations, Lease Renewals and Rent Reviews.  Paul previously worked for the Valuation Office Agency and Januarys in Cambridge.  He has an in depth knowledge of Landlord and Tenant Law and is often called upon to give expert advice in Court.  Paul is a lover of the great outdoors and can often be found hiking in places Gordon has never heard of.

Gary Hubbard is responsible for facilities administration within Merrifields. Gary previously worked in civil engineering and for specialist consultancy services in the construction industry. Outside of work Gary is an active member of his parish council, a qualified shooting instructor and Chairman of a local shooting club.

Mark Kohler is a Graduate Surveyor and currently assists Rowan and Gordon in the Commercial Agency Department. Mark grew up in Somerset. He moved to Bedfordshire to play rugby and study at University. After completing his degree in 2011 he continued a professional rugby career for 6 years in various locations around the country but has now settled in Bury St Edmunds and is enrolled on a Masers Degree in Real Estate.
